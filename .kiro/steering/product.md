# NovaLedger AI

An AI-powered expense tracking and accounting assistant app that uses visual AI (Nova) to analyze receipts and automatically calculate tax-deductible amounts.

## Core Features

- **Receipt Scanning**: Camera-based receipt capture with AI analysis
- **Smart Deduction Logic**: Automatically calculates deductible amounts based on tax rules (e.g., meals 50% deductible, alcohol 0%)
- **Voice Input**: Speech-to-text for expense logging
- **Location Tracking**: Geolocation for expense context
- **Calendar Integration**: Schedule and track expense-related events
- **Chat Interface**: Conversational AI for expense queries
- **Safe Layer Sync**: AWS-backed ledger synchronization

## Design Philosophy

- **Glassmorphism UI**: Frosted glass aesthetic with neon accents (teal and purple)
- **Nova Trace**: Real-time AI reasoning display showing thought process
- **Proactive Intelligence**: Context-aware suggestions and monitoring
